Compost, Recycle, and Rubbish Poster Project

This project is intended to allow you to customize a printable poster design to inform your local populace, whether they be family members, housemates, students, faculty, visitors, employees, or anyone else of the guidelines for recycling, composting, or throwing items away at your location.
